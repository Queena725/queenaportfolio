Practice of Love

2025

Publication

Love is not a feeling we “fall” into; it is a skill we practise. Practice of Love is a pocket-size deck of 24 cards that slows the reader down: each card pairs a short, vertical-set quote with circular sunflower motifs—symbols of the “greatest love”—and invites the reader to shuffle, flip, reorder and reflect. Hand-written typefaces (Altesse Std & AnnabelleJF) keep the tone intimate, while saddle-stitch binding and braided-string closure turn the act of opening the deck into a small ritual of care. Printed on feather-weight stock so the object itself feels as light as the curiosity it hopes to spark.
